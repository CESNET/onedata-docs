---
title: "Request a Space in Onedata"
---

A Space is your project workspace in Onedata. It is where you store, share, and manage data. Spaces are not created automatically — you must either be invited to an existing Space or request a new one.

## Prerequisites

Before requesting a Space, make sure you have:

- An active e-INFRA CZ account.
- A clear idea of how much storage capacity you need.
- Basic project information ready (lead, members, organization, description).

> **Note:** If you are joining an existing project, ask the Space owner to share it with you instead of requesting a new one.

---

## Procedure

Send an email to [onedata@e-infra.cz](mailto:onedata@e-infra.cz) with the following details.

### Email template

```
Subject: Onedata — Space request

Hello Team,

I request an Onedata Space with a capacity of <space_capacity> GB.

Project lead:     <project_lead>
Project members:  <project_members>
Organization:     <organization_name>
Project name:     <project_name>
Project description:

<project_description>
```

Replace the placeholders:
- `<space_capacity>` — estimated storage size in GB
- `<project_lead>` — name and contact of the responsible person
- `<project_members>` — list of team members who need access
- `<organization_name>` — your institution or lab
- `<project_name>` — short name for the Space
- `<project_description>` — brief summary of what the data is for

---

## What happens next

After submitting your request, the Onedata team will review the details and provision your Space. You will receive a confirmation email once the Space is ready.
